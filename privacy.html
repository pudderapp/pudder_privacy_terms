Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026
This policy applies to the Pudder iOS app and Apple Watch companion app.

1. Overview

Pudder is a golf round-tracking app. We designed it with privacy as a default: all your data is stored only on your device. We do not operate servers, create user accounts, or transmit your personal information to any third party.

2. Data We Collect and Why

📍 Location Data On-device only

Pudder uses your precise GPS location during an active round to record where each shot lands and calculate distances to the green. Location is accessed only while a round is in progress and is never uploaded, shared, or used for advertising.

👤 Profile (Name & Handicap) On-device only

You may optionally enter your name and handicap index. This information is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage and is never sent off your device.

🏌️ Round & Shot History On-device only

Your round history, scores, shot positions, club distances, and handicap records are stored entirely on your device. You can export this data as a backup file at any time and delete it permanently from within the app.

🗺️ Course Map Data Public data, anonymous

When you search for a golf course, Pudder queries OpenStreetMap's Overpass API to retrieve publicly available course layout data (hole positions, tees, greens). This request contains no personal information — only the geographic bounding box of the course. Course data is cached on your device to minimise repeat requests.

3. Data Summary

Data Type Where Stored Shared With Third Parties Used for Advertising

4. Apple Watch Companion App

The Pudder Apple Watch app communicates directly with your iPhone over WatchConnectivity. No data passes through external servers. The Watch app uses a temporary HealthKit workout session solely to maintain GPS accuracy during your round — no health or workout data is written to or read from the Health app.

5. Analytics and Tracking

Pudder contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting services. We do not track you across other apps or websites. Apple's own aggregated App Store metrics may be collected by Apple under their own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device until you delete it. You can delete all data at any time from within the app:

  • Delete a single round: Swipe left on any round in your history.
  • Clear all round history: Profile → Data → Clear All Round History.
  • Delete all data: Profile → Data → Delete Account removes your profile, all rounds, club settings, and cached course data permanently.

Because all data is stored only on your device, uninstalling the app will also remove all associated data.

7. Children's Privacy

Pudder is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Because all data stays on the user's device and is never transmitted to us, there are no additional risks specific to children.

8. Third-Party Services

Pudder uses one external service:

  • OpenStreetMap Overpass API (overpass-api.de) — used anonymously to fetch publicly available golf course layout data. No personal data is sent. See the OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy.

Apple Maps (MKLocalSearch) is used for course search. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

9. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store your data on any server, standard data subject rights (access, rectification, portability, erasure) are exercised directly on your device using the deletion features described in Section 6. There is no account to request deletion of from our side — your data belongs entirely to you.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
